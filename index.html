DIAGNOSTIC ARCHIVES

Frequently Queried Parameters

Common questions concerning our GCC trading, warehousing, and customs clearance procedures.

How does CAPTN OS accelerate GCC customs clearance?

We leverage digital APIs to pre-file HS customs classifications and obtain duty-exemption codes in Bahrain's Bayan system before vessels touch Khalifa Bin Salman Port. This ensures instant terminal releases.

What is your minimum container load sourcing volume?

For bulk construction materials and FMCG food items, we coordinate Full Container Loads (FCL). However, for electronics and specialty industrial spares, we operate Less-than-Container Load (LCL) consolidation programs to optimize costs.

How do you verify supplier plant quality?

Our audit desk requests ISO, CE, and GCC conformity certifications. Additionally, we contract third-party inspectors (like SGS) to run physical pre-loading checks on batch quantities.
