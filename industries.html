Logistical Integration

Diverse Sourcing Verticals

CAPTN OS adapts sourcing parameters, custom tariffs, and delivery timelines to accommodate different industry constraints.

FMCG & Consumer Goods

We supply retail grocery networks and wholesale distributors with fast-moving consumer goods, including personal toiletries, household detergents, cosmetics, and packaged paper goods. We prioritize high-turnover brands and ensure secure container transit.

Food & Beverage

Meeting GCC food safety directives is critical. We source grains, vegetable oils, dairy products, canned vegetables, and confectionery. We manage cold-chain shipping logistics to maintain structural integrity and freshness from port to distribution point.

Construction Materials

Supporting Bahrain's architectural and infrastructure growth, we supply steel rebars, structural beams, bulk cement clinker, aggregates, gypsum boards, and premium architectural finishing elements matching precise structural engineer specs.

Industrial & Manufacturing

We keep production plants active. We source factory consumables, industrial valves, safety clothing (PPE), abrasive wheels, machinery spare parts, electrical switchgear, and specialty chemical inputs under stringent compliance codes.

Healthcare Products

We supply medical centers and pharmacies with approved wellness consumables, hospital beds, sanitizers, surgical masks, and diagnostics kits. We coordinate clearance and registration with Bahrain's National Health Regulatory Authority (NHRA).

Electronics & Technology

From electrical relays and semiconductor microchips to consumer hardware components, we bridge global silicon hubs with regional tech fabricators and IT wholesale networks, ensuring safe static-free transportation.

Hospitality & Catering

We supply Bahrain's luxury hotels, resorts, and corporate cafeterias with Operating Supplies and Equipment (OS&E). This includes high-grade cotton linens, tableware, room accessories, and commercial kitchen utensils.

Infrastructure & Utilities

We procure electrical transformers, high-tension cabling, marine fenders, sewage treatment filters, and public lighting components for municipal utility networks, coordinating with ministries to satisfy public tenders.
